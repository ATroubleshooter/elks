The Linux-8086 list at vger.kernel.org is the official ELKS mailing list, on which all topics concerning ELKS are discussed.

To subscribe to the ELKS mailing list, send an email to majordomo@vger.kernel.org containing the words subscribe linux-8086 in the body of the email. If you want to unsubscribe from the ELKS mailing list, send an email to majordomo@vger.kernel.org containing the words unsubscribe linux-8086 <your_email_address> in the body of the email. In both cases, the subject line is ignored.
