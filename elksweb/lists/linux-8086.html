Linux-8086@Vger.Kernel.Org

The Linux-8086 list at vger.kernel.org is the official ELKS mailing list, on which all topics concerning ELKS are discussed.

Subscribing

To subscribe to the ELKS mailing list, send an email to majordomo@vger.kernel.org containing the words...

	subscribe linux-8086

...in the body of the email. The subject line is ignored.

Unsubscribing

If you want to unsubscribe from the ELKS mailing list, send an email to majordomo@vger.kernel.org containing the words...

	unsubscribe linux-8086 <your_email_address>

...in the body of the email. The subject line is ignored.

Archive

An archive of the mailing list is maintained at http://rainbow.cs.unipi.gr/linux-8086-list .
