Latest News

  • 13 Jul 2003: ELKS-0.1.3-pre1 released!!!

    After a long period of inactivity, activity has resumed on the ELKS kernel. Various changes relating to debugging ELKS kernel panics have been integrated into this release, along with various tweaks to the spelling of messages displayed by the kernel.

  • 6 Jan 2003: ELKS-0.1.2 released!!!

    All tweaks to date have been integrated into a new release of the ELKS kernel. Note that, to deal with a bug relating to the internal use of the version number, kernel 0.1.1 had to be skipped, so doesn't exist.

  • 6 May 2002: Early ELKS kernels resurface

    Some of the very early ELKS kernels have turned up on the Internet.

  • 1 May 2002: FIRST EVER ELKS BETA!!!

    IT'S OFFICIAL! The first ever Beta release of the ELKS operating system is out and ready for hammering! Furthermore, the more the users hammer it and report bugs back, the happier the developers will be.

  • 4 Mar 2002: ELKS 0.1.0-pre4 released!!!

    The new prerelease is out. For more info check the CHANGELOG.

  • 24 Nov 2001: ELKS 0.1.0-pre3 released!!!

    The new prerelease is out for download. TCP/IP fixes mainly

  • 24 Nov 2001: ELKS 0.1.0-pre2 released!!!

    The new prerelease is out for download. It contains some significant bug fixes.

  • 20 Nov 2001: ELKS 0.1.0-pre1 released!!!

    The ELKS kernel is now considered to be ready to progress to the next stage of development. Accordingly, the developers have released version 0.1.0-pre1 thereof.
