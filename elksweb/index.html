Latest News

  • 24 Feb 2002: ELKS 0.1.0-pre5 released!!!

    The new prerelease is out. The major changes are that the serial port move was completed (part was accidentally omitted, with the result that 0.1.0-pre4 didn't actually work), and strace was both brought up to date and made to compile.

  • 13 Jan 2002: ELKS 0.1.0-pre4 released!!!

    The new prerelease is out. The major change is that the serial ports moved on the target system, necessitating a new version.

  • 24 Nov 2001: ELKS 0.1.0-pre3 released!!!

    The new prerelease is out for download. TCP/IP fixes mainly

  • 24 Nov 2001: ELKS 0.1.0-pre2 released!!!

    The new prerelease is out for download. It contains some significant bug fixes.

  • 20 Nov 2001: ELKS 0.1.0-pre1 released!!!

    The ELKS kernel is now considered to be ready to progress to the next stage of development. Accordingly, the developers have released version 0.1.0-pre1 thereof.
