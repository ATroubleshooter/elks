The ELKS mailing list

The linux-8086 list at vger.rutgers.edu is the official ELKS mailing list, on which all topics concerning ELKS are discussed.

To subscribe to the ELKS mailing list, send a message to majordomo@vger.rutgers.edu containing the words subscribe linux-8086 in the body.

If you want to unsubscribe the ELKS mailing list, send a message to majordomo@vger.rutgers.edu containing the words

unsubscribe linux-8086 your_email_address

in the body.

An archives of the mailing list can be found at http://lemur.ecs.soton.ac.uk/~threader/LEMUR_lists.cgi.
