ELKS People,

Welcome to your first official report on the status on BlueBell 8086
Linux, which has progressed to Alpha 5 stage! Its currently half workable,
boasting the following commands and utilities. 

bc - the binary calculator, by the FSF. Works, but slightly buggy. Bug is
to do with console and tty drivers.. will be fixed very soon. 

tail - we've ported the world's favourite tailer! :-) tail works great,
and uses libc. 

cut - we've now got a working version of cut. 100% bug free, we're proud.

du - disk usage. works now on ELKS' minix type filesystem. 

grep - a version of grep for your use!

Below is a listing of all of the utilities included in the current Alpha 5
edition of Bluebell, enjoy!
