Where can I find ELKS

ftp://linux.mit.edu/pub/ELKS/ is the current primary ftp site. As it's been down for a while lately, we might change to a different primary site.

ELKS and Dev86 (which is needed to compile ELKS under Linux [see the ELKS-FAQ for details]) can also be found on several other ftp sites:

ftp://ftp.ecs.soton.ac.uk/pub/elks/
ftp://cam053212.student.utwente.nl/pub/8086-nix/ELKS/
ftp://tsx-11.mit.edu/pub/linux/ALPHA/linux-16/
ftp://src.doc.ic.ac.uk/packages/linux/tsx-11-mirror/ALPHA/linux-16/
ftp://ftp.amscons.com/pub/mirrors/ELKS/
